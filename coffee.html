Types of Coffee

Espresso

Espresso is a full-flavored, concentrated form of coffee that is served in "shots."

Latte

A latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk.

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a coffee-based drink made primarily from espresso and milk.

Americano

An Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water.
